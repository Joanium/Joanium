Privacy Policy
Privacy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

🛡️ openworld has no backend. There is no openworld account, no openworld server, and no telemetry. The App runs entirely on your device.

We built openworld because we believe your conversations with AI should be private by default — not monetized, not analyzed, not stored somewhere you don't control.

Your Data

What We Collect — And Don't

  • Conversations — Stored only in local storage on your device. Never sent to us.
  • API keys — Stored only on your device. Never transmitted externally by the App.
  • Your name — Used for personalized greetings. Stored locally, never transmitted.
  • Usage analytics — None. We have no analytics, no crash reporting, no telemetry.
  • Cookies — None. We don't set any cookies.

When you send a message, it goes directly from your device to the AI provider's API (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic). openworld is not an intermediary — it facilitates that direct connection.

Each AI provider has their own privacy policy governing how they handle your prompts. We strongly encourage you to review them.
